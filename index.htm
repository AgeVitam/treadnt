This game requires HTML5 and Javascript.

Instructions:

  • Press a key to slither counterclockwise, release it to slither clockwise.
  • ESC to pause.
  • Hit the boots with your head to bite them and live free.
  • Let the boots tread upon any other part of you to die.
  • True patriot sneks also die if they leave their country or game area.
  • Treadn't believes in your high score being whatever you say it is, patriot.
